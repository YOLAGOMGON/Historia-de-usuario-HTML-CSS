The panda, giant panda, or panda bear is a species of mammal in the order Carnivora. Native to China, its diet is primarily herbivorous, as it feeds almost exclusively on bamboo.
The squirrel is a rodent belonging to the family Sciuridae, which includes ground squirrels, flying squirrels, and tree squirrels. They are known for their agility and ability to climb trees, as well as for their long, bushy tails.
Turtles, or chelonians, are an order of reptiles characterized by a short, wide body and a shell that protects their internal organs. They are cold-blooded animals and can live in both aquatic and terrestrial environments.
